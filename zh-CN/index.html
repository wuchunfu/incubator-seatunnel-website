System Architecture

Apache SeaTunnel is a next-generation high-performance, distributed, and massive data integration framework. Through our efforts to make the use of Spark simpler and more efficient, and solidify the high-quality experience of the industry and our use of Spark into the product SeaTunnel, it significantly reduces the cost of learning and accelerates the deployment of distributed data processing capabilities in the production environment.

Core Features

Rich Components

Built-in rich plugins to support the transmission and integration of various data products

High Scalability

Modularization and plugin, support hot swap, bring better scalability

Easy to use

Unique architecture design,make development and configuration easier

Mature & stable

Experience the baptism of large-scale production environment use and massive data

Apache SeaTunnel (incubating) is an effort undergoing incubation at The Apache Software Foundation (ASF), sponsored by Incubator. Incubation is required of all newly accepted projects until a further review indicates that the infrastructure, communications, and decision making process have stabilized in a manner consistent with other successful ASF projects. While incubation status is not necessarily a reflection of the completeness or stability of the code, it does indicate that the project has yet to be fully endorsed by the ASF.
